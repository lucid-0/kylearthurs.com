Business and Finance

It's important to me to read financial newsletters and keep up with a few youtube channels that follow markets and provide personal finance tips. My parents always set a great example in how to be frugal and intelligent with finances to make a decent living regardless of income level. I've been enjoying learning a little more about investing in the last few years. I generally prefer lower-risk, longer-term, more passive investing. In time I might add some more investment content to this page.
