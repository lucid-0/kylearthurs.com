History

I have been interested in computing since the mid-1990s. Living in rural WI with limited access to technology and connectivity, my family got our first Packard Bell PC running MS DOS and later a Compaq PC with Windows 98 and a 56k modem. As a hobby in high school, I repaired and sold computers that I bought at thrift stores. I also created primitive games and programs using Microsoft's Visual Basic, web technology, and eventually Linux and Python. After I decided to switch majors from business management and finance to computer science, I completed two internships and a university practicum, and graduated to working full time as a software developer.
